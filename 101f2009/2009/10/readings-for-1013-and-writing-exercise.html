Readings for 10/13 and writing exercise

By David Sanson on October 9, 2009 12:18 PM | No Comments

For next Tuesday, read:

  • Chisholm, Roderick, “Human Freedom and the Self”
  • Van Inwagen, Peter (2008), “The Consequence Argument” (note: this pdf contains both “The Consequence Argument” and “The Mystery of Metaphysical Freedom”. We will read both, but for next time, I want you to focus on the first, shorter, piece.)

For your writing exercise, due in class next Tuesday, I’d like you to, in 330 words or less, present the Consequence Argument in its strongest and clearest form, clearly indicating the role played by what van Inwagen calls the “Conditional Rule”. Finally, briefly indicate what you take to be the strongest possible criticism of the argument.

This exercise will not be pass/fail. It will be graded.
