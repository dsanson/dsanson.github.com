Supplementary Readings

I’ll put links to other papers you might be interested in here.

McTaggart

  • Geach P (1995). “Cambridge Philosophers III: McTaggart.” Philosophy 70(274) :567–579 
    • This combines a bit of biography with a sketch of McTaggart’s philosophical system.
  • McTaggart JME (1924). “An Ontological Idealism.” In Muirhead JH, ed., Contemporary British Philosophy: Personal Statements. G. Allen and Unwin. 
    • This is McTaggart’s contribution to a volume that aimed, in the editor’s words, “to give the contributors an opportunity of stating authentically what they regard as the main problem of philosophy and what they have endeavored to make central in their own speculation upon it” (p. 10). McTaggart’s response is to provide a succinct outline of his metaphysical system and what motivates it. and motivations.
  • McTaggart JME (1909). “The Relation of Time and Eternity.” Mind 18(71) :343–362 
    • Wherein McTaggart argues that the last moment of the C-series is eternal. Nice for getting a better sense of how McTaggart’s positive views go.

Russell

  • Russell, B (1903). Sec. 442 of Ch. LIV of Principles of Mathematics.
    • Russell’s account of change circa 1903.

Responses to McTaggart

  • Dummett, M. (1960). “A Defense of McTaggart’s Proof of the Unreality of Time.” The Philosophical Review, 69(4), 497—504.
