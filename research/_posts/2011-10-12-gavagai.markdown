## Gavagi Gavagi

> Gavagai Gavagai,\
> Willard van Orman Quine,\
> Poked at a part of a\
> Rabbit that bit.
> 
> "Entification of\
> Undetached rabbit parts\
> Should be at arm's length," he\
> Said in a snit.
